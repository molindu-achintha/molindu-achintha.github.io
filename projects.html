  • 3D WGAN-GP with lightweight 3D-MDRN generator
  • Novel perceptual loss comparison (Swin UNETR vs DINOv3)
  • Robust preprocessing pipeline with ANTsPy and MONAI
  • Trained on HCP, IXI, and BraTS datasets
